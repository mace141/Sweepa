Sweepa

About

  • Sweepa is a robot vacuum that "cleans" your floor and uses search algorithms to return to its home. You can draw your room's layout, place Sweepa anywhere in the room and watch it clean with different settings.

Help

  • Click and drag on the grid to place down walls. Click the Sweepa button and place Sweepa down by clicking on the grid. Choose a docking mode and press Start to watch Sweepa clean your room!
  • Dijkstra's Algorithm
  • A* Search (not implemented yet)
  • Greedy Best First Search

Cleaning Mode: Random | Docking Algorithm: Dijkstra's Algorithm
